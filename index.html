About us

The story began two years ago, when Katya, on her way home, could not pass "Korsa", this is our first dog, she took her home as an adult, 5-6 years old. Then the OLX helped, We took animals from the street for a while until someone wrote or called.

So we got 3 dogs, 2 cats. When the apartment became too small, We started looking for a bigger place, because there were already 5 dogs. When "Alaska" was given away, we met —Åynologist Oleksyi and his wife Lesya, she is a veterinarian, now she takes care of our dogs and cats
